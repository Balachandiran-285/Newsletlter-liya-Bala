Kiya.ai participates in the FinTech Innovation Challenge Finale

Kiya.ai participated in the FinTech Innovation Challenge Finale, where team Kiya.ai addressed Challenge Statement #1: Digital Identity Management & E-KYC to support the growth & application of #FinTech across the Isle of Man.

The Innovation Challenge is part of the Isle of Man's ambitions to build a secure, vibrant, and sustainable future. It is developed to allow innovative businesses worldwide to experience the island's collaborative financial services sector and showcase their solutions to core challenges identified across the Isle of Man business community.

Kiya.ai's Identity Management solution is designed to streamline and secure the identity verification process for businesses. The solution offers a comprehensive lifecycle that covers all aspects of identity management, from client onboarding to secure storage of digital identities and more.
